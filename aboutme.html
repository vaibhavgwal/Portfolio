About Me

vaibhav agarwal, Industrial Designer

I am a 22 year old product designer, currently in my final year at Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee.

I love exploring internal mechanism of different products and implementing them for making future products. As I have suffered a lot as being differently abled, it would be my endeavor to give comfort to the life of others with my experiences (gained with difficulties) & by way of innovative ideas/product designing.

Have a look at my portfolio and let me know what you think! You can also shoot me an e-mail if you just want to talk. Cheers!

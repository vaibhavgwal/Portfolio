About Me

Hello, my name is Vaibhav Agarwal. I am 23 and currently pursuing Masters in Design from Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi. I have done my undergratuation in Production and Industrial Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee

I love exploring the internal mechanisms of different products and improving them for making future products. As I have suffered a lot as being differently abled, it is my endeavor to give comfort to the life of others with my experiences (gained with difficulties) & by way of innovative ideas/product designing.

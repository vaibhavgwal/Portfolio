Plastic toothbrushes & the pollution that comes with it

Plastic pollution is a common phenomenon that somehow eludes our attention when it shouldn’t. Because as we speak, there are over 8 million pieces of plastic that are being dumped into our oceans from around the world. Due to this, over 100,000 marine mammals and a million sea birds are killed off annually. As a matter of fact, even our toothbrushes, which are made of plastic, have a hand in harming the environment.

My delta creative idea towards minimizing the pollution

Plastic wastes can be reduced if we only replace the wearing part of devices like of brushes, floor mops and other routine consumer products.

This step can help to save plastic pollutants in the atmosphere to great extent.
